What New Technologies Have Emerged?

TARDEC’s (Tank Automotive Research Development and Engineering Center) autonomous trucks. As the name implies, these trucks can work on their own and deliver supplies safely, wherever it may need to be. These vehicles have many different functions and features that they can use while in the field, such as remote-operation.

HUGIN, an AUV (autonomous underwater vehicle), mainly used by militaries for reconnaissance, surveillance, and intelligence. It has three different input modes that allow for different functions, such as being fully autonomous, partially autonomous, and supervised, meaning that it provides real-time data while on a mission.

Predator Drone, a Remotely Piloted Aircraft System (RPAS), an offensive weapon used during the War on Terrorism, which was terrifyingly effective, to both enemies and civilians. While these weren’t fully autonomous, they can easily be made pretty close to being just that, and considering the civilian casualties it has caused, who knows the damage it could have caused if it really were automotive?
